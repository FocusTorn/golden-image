# DISM COMMANDS FOR WINPE

--- CAPTURE (BACKUP) ---
dism /Capture-Image /ImageFile:"OS_BACKUP.wim" /CaptureDir:C:\ /Name:"PreDebloatBackup" /Compress:max

--- RESTORE (APPLY) ---
dism /Apply-Image /ImageFile:"OS_BACKUP.wim" /Index:1 /ApplyDir:C:

--- FIX BOOT (IF NEEDED) ---
bcdboot C:\Windows

--- DRIVE LETTER CHECK ---
If C: is not your Windows drive in WinPE, use:
diskpart
list volume
exit
